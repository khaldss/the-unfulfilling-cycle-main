January felt like an even deeper emptiness than december as it wasnt the kind that comes after spending too much but instead it was the kind of emptiness that came from having nothing to really spend at all. There wasnt any desire pulling me toward anything, no obsession to distract me and really just nothing new that could make me feel happy or excited. It was a very quiet month where everything felt paused as most of my time was spent doing work and preparing for the second term of university as i knew that was when my routine would return. There wasnt much spending, just the basics but when the holiday ended and university started again the repition came back with it. The same travel, the same habits and the same cycle picked up exactly where it left off.
