My project known as The Unfulfilling cycle is a personal project that explores my life in consumersism and how consumerism in general shapes my emotions everyday. My project follows the five stages that I believe that is constantly found when moving through consumersim which is Desire, obsession, happines, emptiness, and repeat. Each page includes drawings of items from my life and these items are things that I have wanted, obssesed over, made me happy, made me regret, and repeatedly return to. When you click on an item you will see a short personal text on what that item represents for me.
